Hobby Classes

  • Home
  • Hobby Classes

Students are encouraged to select one hobby from Dance, Music, Art , Judo, Taekwondo and GYM under the guidance of professional mentors. Dedicated rooms with all modern equipments are provided for students to hone their competencies in these fields.


Music

     a. Guitar Classes

      b. Keyboard Classes

      c. Vocal Classes


Dance

The Students are trained by expert trainers for dance to participate in various programmes and competitions. Dance Room is fully equipped with Modern Sound System and Large mirror to observe body movement.


Judo

Special training on Judo is given under the assistance of an expert Male and a Female trainers. Judo is a Japanese martial art, characterized by its emphasis on head-height kicks, jumping spinning kicks, and fast kicking techniques.


Taekwondo

Special training on Taekwondo is given under the assistance of an expert trainer. Taekwondo is a Korean martial art, characterized by its emphasis on head-height kicks, jumping spinning kicks, and fast kicking techniques.


Art & Craft

Art & Craft classes are conducted here through visuals under the supervision of an expert trainer.


Gymnasium

The school has its own gym to promote physical fitness because a healthy mind rests in a healthy body . Students and staff have access to various Gym equipments under the supervision of our trained, full-time gym instructor.


Yoga

Yoga has been an integral part of health and physical education. The school provides yoga classes to contribute to the physical development of the child and to put a positive impact on psycho social and mental development. It thus contributes to the overall development of the child improving muscular fitness and correcting postures.
